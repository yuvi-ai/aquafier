How it works

From sensor to shutoff, in four steps

01

Sense

Each node's ESP32 reads pH, TDS, temperature, and turbidity sensors submerged in its tank every few seconds.

02

Evaluate

Readings are checked against safe-drinking thresholds locally, and the node's own OLED shows a live SAFE / UNSAFE verdict.

03

Report

Each node sends its status to the master ESP32 over Wi-Fi, which aggregates all three into one system view.

04

Act

If any node is unsafe, the master triggers the relay module, closing the solenoid valve and stopping water flow to the outlet.
